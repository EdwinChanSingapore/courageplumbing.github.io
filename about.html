Courage Plumbing

was set up in the year 2000 by Chan Peng Yong. He started out in plumbing working for an established plumbing company in 1980, rising from a skilled technician(plumbing) to become an assistant manager. He then set out to build his own business. He has been in the Singaporean plumbing industry for over 30 years, and has a diverse experience in plumbing projects. Past projects include the Commercial Locations, including Singapore Zoological Garden and Community Centers Landed properties, including those at Sentosa Cove HDB/Condominum housing and renovation such as the Ladyhill condominium Factories, including 3M Tuas and BMW factory at Kallang Shophouses, including those around Jurong Point Restaurants, including shops in Macritchie Resivour and near punggol park

Courage Plumbing seeks to provide reliable and trusted plumbing services for its customers. Customer satisfaction is of utmost important measure of performance to us, and we strive to provide customisable solutions based on your needs. Before any project, we will discuss with your needs and requirements to ensure that we can deliver high-quality service.
